Today I Ate

According to the WWF site, "...to reduce our ecological footprint our actions must address the sources of the biggest impacts we humans have on our planet: energy use and eating."

Today I Ate is a community food diary app, where people can share tips/ideas of foods to make or recommendations of restaurants to go to. The app generates easy-to-understand infographics of what users ate the past month, in hopes they optimize their eating habits for reducing their ecological footprint.

This app was designed for a Shopify design challenge.
