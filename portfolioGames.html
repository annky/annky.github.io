PC/Unity Mobile Beta Augmented Reality

The Spellbook

A 2D, augmented reality platformer that involves the use of a real life, physical book as a controller. The pages in the book corresponds with "spells" that the player must use to navigate through the game. The game was created in Unity with the help of an AR program called EasyAR. It was displayed at Toronto's Level Up Showcase 2018.

Board Game Prototype

The Things Your Pets Do When You're Sleeping

This is a co-operative Tower Defense style board game where players play as the domesticated house pets of a loving owner. Without his knowledge, the pets defend him every night from waves of ghosts and ghouls who want to find a new host body! However, can they keep their owner safe long enough for the sun to rise? Or will the ghosts have a new body to play with?

This project was a quick design challenge, thus board game pieces were used from the game Betrayal At House on the Hill.
